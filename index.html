Welcome to The Aborigin's

Our Little Story

The Aborigin's Resto and Bar provides a wide range of cuisines from barbecue Grill to Australian's specialities and Indonesian dishes.
We provide comfortable seating in our garden or indoor. The staff is very friendly and always up for a chat to the easygoing guests.
It’s also a great place to enjoy live music from Wednesday till Saturday or for a happy-hour drink at our bar till 7pm.


Free of charge: Large parking space, Pool billiard table & fast Wi-fi hotspot!
